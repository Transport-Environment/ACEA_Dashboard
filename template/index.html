Plugged in: T&E's EV market watch

In 2026, battery electric vehicle sales reached record levels across Europe, with 23 out of 27 EU countries achieving their highest-ever yearly BEV market share. On average, the BEV share increased by two percentage points compared with 2025, rising from 17.4% to 19.4%. Denmark stands out in particular, reaching 80% market share, an increase of 12 percentage points in one year.

In quarter 1 2026, battery electric vehicles in the EU had a market share of 19%.

This dashboard is updated every quarter and now includes Q1 2026 data.
